About Us

We're just a bunch of people who like math, and want to show others how interesting it is.

We have meetings every Monday from 6-7 PM (18:00-19:00) in CDS 548, as well as Putnam problem solving sessions every Thursday from 5-7 PM (17:00-19:00), also in CDS 548. Swing by to learn something new, or just to chat and have pizza.

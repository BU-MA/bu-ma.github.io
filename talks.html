A Friendly Introduction to Abstract Nonsense

Speaker: Grant Talbert

Date: September 22, 2025

Category theory is a unifying language for mathematics, which explains striking connections between distant branches of math. Categories have become foundational to modern study of many mathematical disciplines, especially algebraic disciplines. In this talk, we introduce the basic notions of categories, functors, natural transformations, and universal constructions, guided by examples from basic set theory and algebra. Basic knowledge of sets and functions is recommended.
